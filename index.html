Connect a Google Sheet

The bank pulls live from a Google Sheet. Paste either a Publish-to-web CSV link or a normal share link — the page will figure out the rest.

  1. In your sheet: File → Share → Publish to web, pick the tab, choose Comma-separated values (.csv), Publish.
  2. Paste that link above. A plain /edit link also works if the sheet is shared "anyone with the link can view".
  3. Columns: Section, Entry, Bullet, Tags, Number, Source, Best, Notes.
